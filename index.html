Jacob Coblentz

Bio

I’m a social scientist who studies applying modern compuational methods to complex political problems. I’m currently Chief Data Scientist at Redistricting Partners. Other hats I’ve worn since undergrad have included:

Data Scientist at Afiniti

Special Projects Analyst at Elizabeth Warren For President (A righteous fight I’m particularly proud of)

Analyst at Clarity Campaign Labs

Research Support Associate at the MIT Election Data and Science Lab

In addition I’ve consulted for various groups about implementing simulation based models, program evaluation and coming up with innovative data engineering solutions. I’m also highly experienced in communicated analytic results, with my writing being featured in Data For Progress.

Education

Massachusetts Institute of Technology | Cambridge, MA

Graduate Coursework in Statistics | September 2017 - May 2018

The George Washington University | Washington, DC

B.S. in Mathematics | September 2013 - May 2017
